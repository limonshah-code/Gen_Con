## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Foundation For Casper Mattress

Choosing the right foundation is just as important as choosing the mattress itself. A proper base ensures you get the comfort and support you paid for and can significantly extend the life of your mattress. Here are the key factors to consider.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First and foremost, ensure the foundation is the correct size for your mattress (e.g., Queen, King). Beyond that, check its compatibility with your existing bed frame. Most foundations, like the ones reviewed here, are designed to sit inside a standard bed frame (like a headboard/footboard combo) or on a simple metal frame. If you have a platform bed with built-in slats, you may not need an additional foundation, but always check your mattress warranty requirements. Casper mattresses, like most foam mattresses, require slats to be no more than 4-5 inches apart to prevent sagging.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($100-$300)
Foundations for a Casper mattress can range in price from around $100 for a basic steel model to over $300 for a premium, brand-specific wooden one. A budget-friendly option like the Amazon Basics Smart Box Spring can provide excellent support for around $100-$150.
