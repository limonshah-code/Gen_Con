## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Gas Range Tops 2

Choosing the right product for your gas range involves more than just picking the first one you see. Whether it's a protective cover, a liner, or a portable cooktop, considering a few key factors will ensure you get the best performance, fit, and value for your money.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, identify your primary goal. Are you trying to protect your new stove from spills? Cover up an older, stained surface? Or add portable cooking capacity? For protective covers and liners, compatibility is paramount. Measure your stovetop and burner layout carefully.
