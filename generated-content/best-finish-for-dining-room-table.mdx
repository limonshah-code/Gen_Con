## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Finish For Dining Room Table

Choosing the right dining table involves more than just picking a style. The finish and construction are paramount to its longevity and appearance. Here are the key factors to consider to ensure you make a wise investment.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you start shopping, assess your specific needs. How many people do you need to seat regularly? Consider both your immediate family and how often you entertain guests. Measure your dining space carefully, leaving at least 36 inches of clearance around the table for chairs and movement. Think about the table's primary use. Will it be for formal dining only, or will it double as a homework station or craft area? A table for heavy daily use needs a more durable, low-maintenance finish.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
Dining table prices can range from a few hundred to several thousand dollars. Set a realistic budget based on the materials you prefer. Tables with MDF or veneer tops, like those from GarveeHome, typically fall in the $200-$500 range and offer great value.
